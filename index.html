Common Help

  • Make sure Location Services are enabled if route maps or distance tracking are not working.
  • Make sure Health permissions are enabled if workouts, heart rate, steps, or Apple Watch data are missing.
  • If Apple Watch sync does not appear, open the app on both iPhone and Apple Watch, then try again.
  • For route planning, check your start location, distance target, and route provider settings.

Privacy

Marathon Tracker uses health, fitness, and location data only to provide app features such as workout logging, route mapping, route planning, Apple Watch sync, training summaries, progress tracking, and optional workout sharing.

Health data can include workouts, distance, duration, route points, heart rate, steps, calories, and related workout metrics when you grant permission through Apple Health. Location data can be used to record outdoor routes, show your current position, plan routes, and display maps.

Marathon Tracker does not sell your personal data. Data is stored on your device unless you choose to use features that require external services, such as map display, route planning, offline footpath downloads, live sharing, or sharing a workout summary through iOS.

You can change Health and Location permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app.

Third-Party Content and Services

Marathon Tracker may contain, show, or access third-party content and services needed for maps, routes, and workout features. This can include Apple MapKit map content, OpenStreetMap data, OpenRouteService route data, and data imported from Apple Health with your permission.

Map and route data remains subject to the terms and licences of the relevant providers. OpenStreetMap data is available under the Open Database License and should be credited to OpenStreetMap contributors.

  • Apple MapKit: map display, routing, and location-related map features.
  • OpenStreetMap: optional footpath and map-derived route data.
  • OpenRouteService: optional route generation when configured in the app.
  • Apple Health: optional workout, heart rate, steps, route, and fitness data imported with your permission.

Map data attribution: © OpenStreetMap contributors where OpenStreetMap data is used.
